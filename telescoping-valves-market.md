<p><ul><li>Rising Telescoping Valves</li><li>Non-Rising Telescoping Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The telescoping valves market encompasses two primary types: rising and non-rising telescoping valves. Rising telescoping valves extend vertically as they open, allowing for a more straightforward operation in various applications, including irrigation and drainage. Non-rising telescoping valves, on the other hand, maintain a fixed position and operate through lateral movement, making them suitable for limited space environments. Both types are essential in fluid control systems, ensuring efficient flow regulation and versatile installation options across diverse industries.</p></p>
