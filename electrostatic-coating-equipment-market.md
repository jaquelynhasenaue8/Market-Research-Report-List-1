<p><p>The electrostatic coating equipment market is divided into two main types: liquid electrostatic coating equipment and powder electrostatic coating equipment. Liquid electrostatic equipment utilizes liquid paints that are charged to enhance adhesion and reduce overspray, providing a smooth finish. In contrast, powder electrostatic coating equipment uses dry powder that is electrically charged, allowing for an even application and efficient, eco-friendly results. Both types are widely used in various industries for their effectiveness in achieving high-quality coatings with minimal waste.</p></p>
