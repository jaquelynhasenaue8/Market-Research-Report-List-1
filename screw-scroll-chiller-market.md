<p><p>The screw and scroll chiller market comprises two primary types: water-cooled scroll chillers and air-cooled scroll chillers. Water-cooled scroll chillers utilize a cooling tower and water as the heat rejection medium, making them ideal for larger applications with higher efficiency and lower operational costs. In contrast, air-cooled scroll chillers use ambient air for cooling, offering easier installation and lower maintenance, making them suitable for smaller installations or where water availability is limited. Both types play critical roles in HVAC systems.</p></p>
