Conversely, PVC automotive wire is made with polyvinyl chloride insulation, providing good flexibility and insulation properties, commonly used in standard automotive wiring. Both types are essential for ensuring electrical connectivity and reliability within vehicle systems, catering to different vehicle performance and safety requirements.</p></p>
