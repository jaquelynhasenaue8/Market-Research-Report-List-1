<p><p>Safety nets are categorized into three market types based on their application and structure. **Parallel Nets** are horizontal nets used primarily in construction to prevent falls from heights, installed parallel to the ground. **Vertical Nets** are erected vertically, often along building edges or scaffolding, to contain debris or prevent falls sideways. **Mesh Safety Nets** feature a woven mesh design, offering flexibility and durability, commonly used in sports, construction, and industrial settings to ensure safety and containment. Each type serves specific safety needs.</p></p>
