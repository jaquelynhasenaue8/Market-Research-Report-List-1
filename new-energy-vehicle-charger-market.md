<p><strong>The New Energy Vehicle Charger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DC Charging Pile</li><li>AC Charging Pile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) charger market is primarily divided into **DC Charging Piles** and **AC Charging Piles**. DC Charging Piles provide high-power, fast charging, ideal for public stations and long-distance travel, delivering direct current to the vehicle's battery. AC Charging Piles offer slower, lower-power charging, commonly used in residential or workplace settings, supplying alternating current that the vehicle's onboard charger converts. Both types cater to different charging needs, balancing speed, convenience, and infrastructure requirements in the growing NEV ecosystem.</p></p>
