<p><p>The portable breathing machine market includes various types designed to assist patients with respiratory conditions. Asthma nebulizers deliver medication directly to the lungs, providing relief for asthma sufferers. CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) machines are used primarily for sleep apnea, maintaining open airways during sleep. BIPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure) machines offer variable pressure for inhalation and exhalation, suitable for patients with more complex breathing issues. Other devices may include portable oxygen concentrators and advanced ventilators, catering to diverse respiratory needs.</p></p>
