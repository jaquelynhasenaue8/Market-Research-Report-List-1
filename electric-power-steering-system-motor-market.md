<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electric Power Steering System Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS Motor</li><li>REPS Motor</li><li>PEPS Motor</li><li>EHPS Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Power Steering (EPS) system market includes various motor types: CEPS (Column-Assist EPS), REPS (Rack-Assist EPS), PEPS (Pinion-Assist EPS), and EHPS (Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering). CEPS motors are mounted on the steering column, offering compact design and cost efficiency. REPS motors integrate with the steering rack, providing higher torque for larger vehicles. PEPS motors assist at the pinion, balancing performance and space. EHPS combines electric and hydraulic systems, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Each type caters to specific vehicle needs, balancing performance, efficiency, and cost.</p></p>
