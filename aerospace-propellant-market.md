<p><p>The aerospace propellant market is segmented into solid, liquid, gaseous, and other propellants. Solid propellants consist of a mixture of fuel and oxidizer in a solid form, favored for their simplicity and reliability. Liquid propellants are typically composed of separate fuel and oxidizer that are mixed in the combustion chamber, offering flexibility and controlled thrust. Gaseous propellants, often used in certain types of thrusters, rely on gas-phase reactions. Other propellants include advanced options like hybrid systems and environmentally friendly alternatives.</p></p>
