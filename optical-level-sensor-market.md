<p><p>The optical level sensor market is segmented into two primary types: continuous level monitoring and point level monitoring. Continuous level monitoring provides real-time tracking of liquid levels, ensuring ongoing assessment for processes requiring stability, such as in tanks. In contrast, point level monitoring detects specific level thresholds, triggering alarms or actions when a predetermined level is reached, making it ideal for applications like overflow prevention. Both types leverage optical technology for accurate and reliable level detection in various industries.</p></p>
