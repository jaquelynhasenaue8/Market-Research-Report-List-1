<p><p>The high voltage surge arrester market comprises three main types: embedded type, wall-mount type, and built-in type. Embedded type arresters are integrated within electrical equipment for compact protection, ideal for space-constrained applications. Wall-mount type arresters are externally mounted on walls, suitable for easy access and maintenance in larger installations. Built-in type arresters are designed within equipment housings, providing streamlined protection while utilizing minimal space, commonly found in transformers and switchgear to enhance system reliability.</p></p>
