<p><p>The privacy glass market comprises various types, including electrochromic, thermochromic, and others. Electrochromic glass changes opacity when an electrical charge is applied, allowing for adjustable privacy levels. Thermochromic glass, on the other hand, alters its transparency in response to temperature changes, providing privacy without requiring power. Other technologies include suspended particle devices, which adjust visibility using suspended particles within the glass. Each type offers unique benefits for residential, automotive, and commercial applications, enhancing privacy and energy efficiency.</p></p>
