<p><p>Frequency multipliers are electronic circuits used to generate frequencies that are integer multiples of a given input frequency. The market is divided into two main types: active frequency multipliers, which utilize active components like transistors and amplifiers to achieve higher frequencies with improved performance and stability; and passive frequency multipliers, which rely on passive components such as capacitors and inductors, generally offering simpler designs but with lower efficiency. Both types cater to various applications in telecommunications, radar, and signal processing.</p></p>
