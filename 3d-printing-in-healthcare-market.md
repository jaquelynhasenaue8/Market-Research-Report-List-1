<p><strong>The 3D Printing in Healthcare Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extrusion</li><li>Photo-polymerization</li><li>Laser Beam Melting</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>3D printing in healthcare encompasses various market types, including extrusion, photo-polymerization, and laser beam melting. Extrusion involves layering materials like thermoplastics to create custom prosthetics and implants. Photo-polymerization uses light-sensitive resins to craft intricate models and dental applications with high precision. Laser beam melting employs high-powered lasers to fuse metal powders, ideal for creating durable surgical instruments and orthopedic implants. Additionally, other technologies, such as binder jetting and vat polymerization, contribute to diverse healthcare applications, enhancing patient-specific solutions.</p></p>
