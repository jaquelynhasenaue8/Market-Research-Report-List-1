<p><p>Automatic sorting systems, such as tilt-tray and cross-belt systems, are advanced solutions for efficient material handling and logistics. Tilt-tray systems use trays that tilt to discharge items onto designated chutes, ideal for lightweight, irregularly shaped goods. Cross-belt systems feature conveyor belts mounted on carts, enabling precise sorting of heavier or fragile items. Both systems enhance speed, accuracy, and scalability in industries like e-commerce, retail, and parcel delivery, catering to diverse sorting needs while optimizing operational efficiency and reducing manual labor.</p></p>
