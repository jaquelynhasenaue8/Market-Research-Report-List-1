<p><p>The Screw Bending Machine market is segmented into three main types: Manual, Electric, and Hydraulic. Manual machines require operator intervention to bend screws and are often used for low-volume tasks. Electric machines automate the bending process, offering precision and efficiency, suitable for medium to high production levels. Hydraulic machines utilize pressurized fluid to perform heavy-duty bending, ideal for high-tier industrial applications that demand significant force and versatility. Each type addresses different operational needs in the metalworking industry.</p></p>
