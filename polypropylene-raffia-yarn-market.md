Twisted yarn is characterized by its helically twisted fibers, providing strength and durability, commonly used in ropes and heavy-duty applications. Spooled yarn is wound onto spools for ease of storage and handling, making it suitable for automated weaving and industrial uses. Each type serves distinct needs in various industries.</p></p>
