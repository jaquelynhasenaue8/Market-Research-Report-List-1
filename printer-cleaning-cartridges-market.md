<p><ul><li>One-piece Ink Cartridge</li><li>Split Type Ink Cartridge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The printer cleaning cartridges market features two main types: one-piece ink cartridges and split-type ink cartridges. One-piece ink cartridges contain both ink and print head in a single unit, simplifying installation and maintenance. In contrast, split-type ink cartridges consist of separate ink tanks and print heads, allowing users to replace only the depleted component, reducing waste and cost. Both types compete in terms of efficiency, compatibility, and price, catering to different consumer preferences and printer models.</p></p>
