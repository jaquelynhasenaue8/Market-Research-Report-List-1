<p><p>PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) coating/plating machines encompass various technologies for applying thin films to surfaces. Evaporation coating machines utilize heat to vaporize materials, allowing them to condense onto substrates, forming a uniform layer. Sputtering film coaters, on the other hand, eject material from a target using high-energy ions, resulting in controlled deposition on surfaces. Additionally, the "Other" market includes alternative PVD technologies like cathodic arc and pulsed laser deposition, catering to specific applications and industry needs.</p></p>
