<p><p>The CVD graphene film market is categorized into three main types: single layer graphene, double layer graphene, and multilayer graphene. Single layer graphene, composed of a single atom-thick sheet, offers exceptional electrical and mechanical properties, making it ideal for electronics and sensors. Double layer graphene consists of two stacked layers, which enhances conductivity and stability. Multilayer graphene, with multiple stacked layers, provides improved mechanical strength and is often used in composites, batteries, and energy storage applications, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
