<p><p>The screen privacy protecting film market comprises primarily two types: tempered glass film and TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) film. In contrast, TPU film is a flexible, lightweight option that effectively shields screens from fingerprints and minor abrasions, while also ensuring privacy. Both types cater to varied consumer needs, balancing protection and aesthetic appeal, making them popular choices in the device accessories market.</p></p>
