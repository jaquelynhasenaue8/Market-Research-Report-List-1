<p><p>LED driver ICs are essential components in the lighting market, categorized primarily into AC and DC market types. AC LED drivers convert alternating current into a regulated output suitable for LEDs, often utilized in indoor and outdoor lighting applications, ensuring stability and efficiency. Conversely, DC LED drivers are designed for direct current systems, commonly employed in battery-powered or low-voltage applications. Both types play vital roles in optimizing LED performance, enhancing energy efficiency, and prolonging the lifespan of lighting systems.</p></p>
