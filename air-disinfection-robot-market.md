<p><p>The Air Disinfection Robot market features two main types: UV Disinfection Robots and HPV Disinfection Robots. UV Disinfection Robots utilize ultraviolet light to neutralize airborne pathogens effectively, making them suitable for environments like hospitals and public spaces. In contrast, HPV (Hydrogen Peroxide Vapor) Disinfection Robots release vaporized hydrogen peroxide to disinfect air and surfaces, offering a more thorough decontamination method. Both technologies aim to enhance indoor air quality and reduce the spread of infectious diseases in various settings.</p></p>
