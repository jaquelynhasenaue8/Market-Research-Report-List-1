<p><p>The ampoules packaging market is primarily divided into two types: glass ampoules and plastic ampoules. Glass ampoules are favored for their inertness, providing strong protection against contamination and preserving the stability of sensitive substances like pharmaceuticals and chemicals. They are also heat-resistant, making them suitable for sterilization. On the other hand, plastic ampoules offer advantages such as lightweight, shatterproof properties, and cost-effectiveness, making them easier to transport and handle. Both types serve diverse applications across various industries, including healthcare and cosmetics.</p></p>
