<p><p>Document Management Software (DMS) encompasses tools for capturing, storing, and managing digital documents. The market is primarily divided into two types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise DMS involves installing the software on local servers, offering greater control and security but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based DMS operates on remote servers, providing accessibility from any location and typically lower upfront costs, while relying on the vendor for maintenance and security. Each type caters to different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
