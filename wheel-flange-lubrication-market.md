<p><p>The wheel flange lubrication market primarily consists of two types: Easy Rail Oil and Easy Rail Grease. Easy Rail Oil is a liquid lubricant designed to reduce friction and wear between wheel flanges and rails, ensuring smoother operations and extended component life. In contrast, Easy Rail Grease provides a thicker, more protective layer, ideal for high-load applications and harsher environments. Both products enhance rail performance, improve energy efficiency, and minimize maintenance efforts for railway operators.</p></p>
