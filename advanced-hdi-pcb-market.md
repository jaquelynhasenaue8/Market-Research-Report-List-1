<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Advanced HDI PCB Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HDI PCB (1+N+1)</li><li>HDI PCB (2+N+2)</li><li>ELIC (Every Layer Interconnection)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Advanced HDI PCB market comprises various types of high-density interconnect boards that enhance electronic performance. The HDI PCB (1+N+1) features a single layer of circuitry on either side of a core substrate, allowing for compact designs. HDI PCB (2+N+2) contains two layers of circuitry on each side, providing higher complexity and interconnections. ELIC (Every Layer Interconnection) supports vertical interconnections between all layers, facilitating advanced designs with increased functionality and reduced size, making them ideal for modern electronic devices.</p></p>
