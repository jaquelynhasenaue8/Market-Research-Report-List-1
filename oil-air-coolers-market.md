<p><strong>The Oil-air Coolers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-type</li><li>Dry-type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil-air coolers market is primarily divided into two types: oil-type and dry-type coolers. Oil-type coolers utilize a heat exchanger to transfer heat from oil to air, effectively cooling lubricating and hydraulic oils in various industrial applications. In contrast, dry-type coolers employ ambient air to dissipate heat without the use of liquid coolant, making them suitable for applications where water cooling is impractical. Both types serve essential roles in maintaining optimal operating temperatures in machinery and equipment.</p></p>
