<p><p>The wheel alignment system market encompasses three primary types: 3D wheel aligners, CCD wheel aligners, and laser wheel aligners. 3D wheel aligners utilize advanced camera technology for precise measurements, offering high accuracy and efficiency. CCD wheel aligners employ charge-coupled device technology, delivering reliable alignment data with ease of use. Laser wheel aligners use laser beams to measure angles and distances, providing quick and exact results. Together, these systems enhance vehicle performance and extend tire life through accurate alignment services.</p></p>
