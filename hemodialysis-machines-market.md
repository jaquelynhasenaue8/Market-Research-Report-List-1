<p><ul><li>Single Pump Hemodialysis Machines</li><li>Double Pump Hemodialysis Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hemodialysis machines market is segmented into single pump and double pump systems. Single pump hemodialysis machines utilize one pump for both blood and dialysate flow, offering a simpler setup and lower cost, making them suitable for home dialysis and smaller clinics. In contrast, double pump machines use separate pumps for blood and dialysate, providing enhanced control over flow rates and improved treatment efficiency, often preferred in larger healthcare facilities for complex cases and higher patient volumes.</p></p>
