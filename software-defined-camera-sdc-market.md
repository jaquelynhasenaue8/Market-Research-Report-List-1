<p><p>Software-Defined Cameras (SDCs) are advanced imaging systems that utilize software to enhance, control, and modify the way images are captured and processed. This adaptability makes them suitable for various industries, including surveillance, automotive, robotics, and consumer electronics.</p><p>The Software-Defined Camera Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.7% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing demand for high-resolution imaging and real-time processing capabilities. The expanding use of IoT devices also facilitates the integration of SDCs into diverse applications, further broadening the market scope.
