<p><strong>The Static VAR Compensator (SVC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thyristor-based</li><li>MCR-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A Static VAR Compensator (SVC) is a device used in electrical power systems to regulate voltage and improve stability by providing reactive power compensation. It typically operates using thyristors or mechanical switching components, which can vary the reactive power output. In a thyristor-based market, SVCs dynamically adjust power levels with fast response times, enhancing grid reliability. Conversely, an MCR-based (Mechanical Switch Capacitor Reactors) market employs slower mechanical methods for reactive power management, offering a more cost-effective solution but with limited response capabilities.</p></p>
