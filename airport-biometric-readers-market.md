<p><ul><li>Fingerprint Readers</li><li>Facial Recognition Readers</li><li>Hand Geometry Readers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airport biometric readers market comprises three main types: fingerprint readers, facial recognition readers, and hand geometry readers. Fingerprint readers utilize unique finger patterns for identification, offering high accuracy and quick processing. Facial recognition readers analyze facial features, enabling contactless identification and a seamless passenger experience. Hand geometry readers measure the shape and size of hands, providing a reliable identification method, particularly in high-security environments. Together, these technologies enhance airport security, expedite passenger processing, and improve overall operational efficiency.</p></p>
