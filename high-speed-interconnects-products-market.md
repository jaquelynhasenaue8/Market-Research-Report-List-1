<p><p>The high-speed interconnects products market encompasses components that facilitate rapid data transmission in networking and data centers. Active Optical Cables (AOC) utilize active transceivers to convert electrical signals to optical signals, enhancing bandwidth and distance capabilities. In contrast, Direct Attach Cables (DAC) are simpler, using copper cabling with fixed connectors for short-distance connections, offering cost-effectiveness and lower latency. Both types are essential for optimizing performance and connectivity in high-demand environments, catering to diverse applications in telecommunications and enterprise networking.</p></p>
