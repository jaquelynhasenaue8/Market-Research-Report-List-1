<p><p>Chip resistors are compact, surface-mount components widely used in electronic circuits for current limiting and voltage division. The market for chip resistors is segmented into pressure-sensitive and thermosensitive types. Pressure-sensitive chip resistors adjust their resistance based on applied pressure, making them suitable for applications like touch sensors. Thermosensitive chip resistors change resistance with temperature fluctuations, ideal for temperature monitoring and control in various devices. Both types cater to specific application needs in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
