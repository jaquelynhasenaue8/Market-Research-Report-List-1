<p><p>Spin-on-carbon hard masks are materials used in semiconductor manufacturing to provide a protective layer during the etching process. These masks are applied in liquid form and spun onto the substrate, forming a thin, uniform layer upon curing. Their advantages include excellent etch resistance, high temperature stability, and the ability to achieve sub-micron features, making them vital in advanced lithography applications in the production of integrated circuits and microelectronic devices.</p><p>The Spin-on-carbon hard mask market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for miniaturization in electronic devices and advancements in semiconductor technology.
