<p><p>The water purification filters market consists of various types designed for effective filtration. Activated carbon filters remove impurities and chlorine, improving taste and odor. PP filters, or polypropylene filters, capture sediments and larger particles. Ceramic filters use porous materials to eliminate bacteria and pathogens. Resin filters soften water by removing minerals like calcium and magnesium. Titanium rod filters enhance purification by providing antimicrobial properties and reducing contaminants. Each type caters to specific needs, ensuring access to clean water.</p></p>
