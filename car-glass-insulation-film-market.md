<p><p>The car glass insulation film market is segmented into solar control and convection control types. Conversely, convection control films focus on minimizing heat transfer through convection currents, improving thermal insulation. Together, these films enhance energy efficiency, reduce reliance on air conditioning, and contribute to a more comfortable driving experience by moderating interior temperatures.</p></p>
