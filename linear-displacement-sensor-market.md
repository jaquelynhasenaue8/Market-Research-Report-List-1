<p><p>The linear displacement sensor market is segmented into contact and non-contact types. Contact type sensors measure displacement through direct physical interaction with the object, often offering high accuracy and resolution, but may wear over time. Non-contact type sensors, on the other hand, utilize technologies like laser or optical systems to measure distance without physical contact. This results in minimal wear and allows for measurement of delicate or high-speed objects, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
