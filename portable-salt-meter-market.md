<p><p>The portable salt meter market includes three primary types: optical, conductivity, and hydrometer. Optical type meters use light refraction to measure salt concentration, providing quick and accurate readings. Conductivity meters measure the electrical conductivity of a solution, which correlates with salt levels, making them ideal for various applications. Hydrometer type meters utilize buoyancy principles to assess salt concentration, offering a simple, cost-effective solution. Each type serves diverse industries, including agriculture, aquaculture, and water quality monitoring, catering to specific measurement needs.</p></p>
