<p><strong>The Cryogenic Condensate Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Displacement Pump</li><li>Power Pump</li><li>Retention Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cryogenic condensate pump market encompasses various types, including positive displacement pumps, power pumps, and retention pumps. Positive displacement pumps move fluid by trapping a fixed volume and forcing it into the discharge, ensuring consistent flow at varying pressures. Power pumps rely on mechanical energy to propel the condensate, typically using centrifugal force, making them suitable for high-capacity applications. Retention pumps, designed to hold and manage fluids in specific areas, help regulate flow rates and maintain system efficiency in cryogenic processes.</p></p>
