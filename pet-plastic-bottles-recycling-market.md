<p><p>The PET plastic bottles recycling market comprises two main types: chemical and mechanical recycling. Chemical recycling involves breaking down PET into its raw materials through processes like depolymerization, allowing for the creation of high-quality recycled PET suitable for food-grade applications. In contrast, mechanical recycling focuses on shredding and melting used PET bottles to produce recycled pellets, which can be remolded into new products. Both methods aim to reduce environmental impact, promote sustainability, and contribute to a circular economy.</p></p>
