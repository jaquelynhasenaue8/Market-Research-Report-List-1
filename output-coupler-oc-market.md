<p><p>Output couplers (OCs) are essential components in laser systems, used to extract a portion of the laser beam while allowing the remainder to continue reflecting within the laser cavity. The types of OCs include plane, concave, and convex markets. Plane OCs provide uniform beam profiles, while concave OCs focus the output, enhancing intensity. Convex OCs, on the other hand, diverge the beam, useful for specific applications like beam shaping. Each type caters to distinct optical requirements in laser applications.</p></p>
