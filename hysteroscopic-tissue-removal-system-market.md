<p><strong>The Hysteroscopic Tissue Removal System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bipolar</li><li>Monopolar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hysteroscopic Tissue Removal System market is segmented into bipolar and monopolar types. Bipolar systems employ two electrodes, allowing for precise tissue removal with reduced thermal spread, minimizing damage to surrounding tissues. In contrast, monopolar systems utilize a single electrode and require grounding pads, which can amplify risks of thermal injury. Both types serve to treat uterine conditions through minimally invasive procedures, with bipolar systems gaining popularity for their safety profile and efficacy in delicate surgeries.</p></p>
