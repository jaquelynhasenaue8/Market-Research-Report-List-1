<p><p>The construction pozzolan market is divided into two main types: artificial pozzolans and natural pozzolans. Artificial pozzolans are manufactured materials, such as fly ash, slag, and silica fume, created from industrial processes that enhance concrete properties and durability. In contrast, natural pozzolans are naturally occurring materials, like volcanic ash and certain clays, known for their cement-like properties when mixed with water. Both types improve the strength and longevity of concrete, making them essential in sustainable construction practices.</p></p>
