<p><p>The medical electrodes market comprises two main types: disposable electrodes and reusable electrodes. Disposable electrodes are designed for single use, ensuring sterility and reducing the risk of cross-contamination, making them ideal for short-term procedures and routine monitoring. In contrast, reusable electrodes are built for multiple applications and can be cleaned and maintained for extended use, offering cost-effectiveness over time. Both types play crucial roles in various medical applications, including electrocardiograms (ECGs), electromyography (EMG), and other diagnostic procedures.</p></p>
