<p><p>The Automotive Electric Cable Market includes PVC Type and Cross-Linked Type cables. Cross-Linked Type cables, often using materials like XLPE, provide higher thermal resistance, durability, and better performance under extreme temperatures and mechanical stress, ideal for high-demand applications. Both types cater to diverse automotive electrical needs, balancing cost, performance, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
