<p><strong>The Process Gas Blower Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Displacement Blower</li><li>Centrifugal Blower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The process gas blower market consists primarily of two types: positive displacement blowers and centrifugal blowers. Positive displacement blowers operate by trapping a fixed volume of gas and forcing it out, making them ideal for high-pressure applications and consistent gas flow. In contrast, centrifugal blowers utilize rotating impellers to impart velocity to the gas, resulting in high flow rates and making them suitable for low-pressure applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, influencing efficiency and operational performance in various processes.</p></p>
