<p><p>The non-contact medical infrared thermometer market is primarily categorized into two types: forehead type and ear type thermometers. Forehead type thermometers measure temperature by scanning the skin's surface, providing quick and non-invasive readings, making them ideal for screening in public settings. Ear type thermometers measure infrared heat from the eardrum, offering accurate and rapid results, often preferred in clinical environments. Both types are widely used for their convenience and efficiency in temperature assessment, especially during health emergencies.</p></p>
