<p><p>The GPS locator market is broadly categorized into real-time location and regular-time location segments. Real-time location systems provide continuous updates, allowing for immediate tracking of assets or individuals, making them ideal for logistics, fleet management, and personal safety applications. In contrast, regular-time location systems offer periodic tracking updates, capturing locations at predefined intervals. This approach is often used in less time-sensitive applications such as wildlife tracking or vehicle monitoring, where continuous data is not critical. Both segments cater to diverse user needs.</p></p>
