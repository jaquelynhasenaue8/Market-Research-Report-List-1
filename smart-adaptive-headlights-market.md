<p><p>Smart Adaptive Headlights (SAH) enhance driving safety by adjusting beam patterns based on road conditions, speed, and steering. The market includes two key types: Adaptive Front-lighting Systems (AFS) and Adaptive Beam Distribution (ABD). AFS adjusts headlight angles and intensity for curves, intersections, and varying speeds, improving visibility. ABD dynamically controls light distribution to avoid glare for oncoming traffic while maximizing illumination. Both technologies optimize nighttime driving, reduce accidents, and comply with safety regulations, driving their adoption in the automotive industry.</p></p>
