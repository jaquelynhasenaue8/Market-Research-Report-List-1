<p><p>Cold chain monitoring data loggers are essential for ensuring the integrity of temperature-sensitive food products during transportation and storage. Offline data loggers collect temperature data during transit, requiring physical retrieval for analysis. Cellular connected data loggers provide real-time monitoring through mobile networks, enabling instant alerts and data access. Wireless connected data loggers use Wi-Fi or Bluetooth for continuous monitoring and can transmit data to cloud platforms, offering enhanced visibility and control over the cold chain process.</p></p>
