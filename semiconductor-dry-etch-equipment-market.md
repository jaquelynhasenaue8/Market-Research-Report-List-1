<p><p>The semiconductor dry etch equipment market encompasses various types, including dielectric, silicon, metal, and other etching processes. Dielectric etching primarily targets insulating materials like silicon dioxide, crucial for circuit layering. Silicon etching focuses on removing silicon to create patterns in chips. Metal etching is essential for defining metallic interconnects, facilitating electrical connections. Other etching processes cater to specialized materials or unique applications. Each type plays a vital role in semiconductor fabrication, enabling the production of advanced electronic devices.</p></p>
