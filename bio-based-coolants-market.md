<p><p>The bio-based coolants market is primarily divided into two types: semi-synthetic and synthetic bio-based coolants. They provide effective heat transfer and lubrication, while being less harmful compared to conventional coolants. Synthetic bio-based coolants, on the other hand, are derived entirely from renewable resources, designed for optimal thermal management and reduced ecological impact. Both types cater to industries seeking sustainable alternatives to traditional coolant solutions.</p></p>
