<p><p>Flame arresters are crucial safety devices used to prevent flames from propagating through pipelines and storage systems. The market is primarily divided into two types: dry-type flame arresters and wet-type flame arresters. Dry-type flame arresters utilize a mesh or screen to extinguish flames by cooling and dissipating heat, making them suitable for gas applications. In contrast, wet-type flame arresters incorporate a liquid barrier to absorb energy, making them effective for handling flammable liquids and vapors in various industrial settings.</p></p>
