<p><strong>The PVC Compound Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Plasticised PVC</li><li>Plasticised PVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVC compound market is primarily divided into two types: non-plasticised PVC (uPVC) and plasticised PVC (PVC-P). Non-plasticised PVC is rigid and commonly used in applications such as pipes, windows, and doors due to its durability and strength. Conversely, plasticised PVC contains added plasticisers, making it flexible and suitable for applications like flooring, cables, and inflatable items. Each type caters to distinct industry needs, leveraging their specific properties for various manufacturing and construction purposes.</p></p>
