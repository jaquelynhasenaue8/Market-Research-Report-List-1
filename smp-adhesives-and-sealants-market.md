<p><p>The SMP adhesives and sealants market can be categorized into one-component and two-component types. One-component SMP products are ready-to-use formulations that cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, providing convenience and ease of application. In contrast, two-component SMP products require mixing of a resin and hardener before application, offering enhanced performance characteristics, such as improved strength and chemical resistance. These types cater to varied industrial applications, emphasizing versatility and effectiveness in bonding and sealing tasks.</p></p>
