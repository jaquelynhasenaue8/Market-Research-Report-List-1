<p><strong>The Semiconductor Wafer Electrostatic Chucks ESC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Semiconductor Wafer Electrostatic Chuck (ESC) market is primarily categorized into two types: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. Coulomb Type ESCs utilize electrostatic forces to hold wafers, offering high stability and minimal thermal expansion, ideal for precision applications. In contrast, JR Type ESCs employ a combination of electrostatic forces and mechanical clamping, providing enhanced grip and uniform heating. Both types are essential for improving wafer processing efficiency in semiconductor manufacturing, supporting advancements in technology and production capabilities.</p></p>
