<p><p>The vibrating mesh nebulizer market is categorized primarily into piezoelectric actuation and other types. Piezoelectric actuation employs piezoelectric materials that convert electrical energy into mechanical vibrations, driving the mesh to produce aerosol particles for inhalation. This technology offers precise medication delivery and minimal waste. Other market types may include ultrasonic and jet nebulizers, which use different mechanisms for aerosol generation. These variations cater to diverse patient needs and preferences, influencing the overall dynamics of the nebulizer market.</p></p>
