<p><p>Weather buoy markets are categorized into solar-powered and battery-powered types. Solar-powered buoys utilize solar panels to harness sunlight, making them environmentally friendly and suitable for long-term deployment in remote areas with ample sunlight. These buoys often require minimal maintenance. In contrast, battery-powered buoys rely on batteries for operation, providing reliable performance regardless of sunlight availability. However, they may require periodic battery replacement or recharging. Both types serve critical roles in maritime weather monitoring and environmental research.</p></p>
