<p><p>The Banking and Payment Smart Cards market comprises two primary types: contact-based and contactless smart cards. Contact-based smart cards require physical insertion into a card reader to facilitate transactions, ensuring secure data transmission through direct contact. In contrast, contactless smart cards utilize RFID or NFC technology, allowing users to complete transactions by simply tapping the card near a reader, enhancing speed and convenience. Both types play a crucial role in enabling secure payments and improving the overall banking experience.</p></p>
