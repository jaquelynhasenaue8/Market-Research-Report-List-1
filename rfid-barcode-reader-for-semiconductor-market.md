<p><p>RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) and barcode readers are essential tools in the semiconductor market for tracking and managing inventory. The CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) method uses an array of tiny sensors to capture images of barcodes, providing fast and accurate scanning in various lighting conditions. In contrast, the laser method employs a laser beam to read barcodes, enabling long-range scanning and high-speed performance. Both technologies enhance operational efficiency by ensuring precise tracking of semiconductor components throughout the supply chain.</p></p>
