<p><ul><li>Reactive Silicone Fluid</li><li>Non-Reactive Silicone Fluid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The modified silicone market is categorized into reactive and non-reactive silicone fluids. Reactive silicone fluids contain functional groups that enable them to chemically bond with substrates, enhancing properties like durability and adhesion in various applications such as coatings, adhesives, and sealants. In contrast, non-reactive silicone fluids are stable and do not undergo chemical changes; they primarily serve as lubricants, anti-foaming agents, and conditioning agents in cosmetics and personal care products, offering benefits without altering their composition.</p></p>
