<p><p>In-Mold Labeling (IML) is a process where labels are applied to a mold before the plastic is injected, blown, or formed, creating a seamless bond between the label and the product. The market comprises three main types: Injection Molding IML, where materials are injected into a mold with the label; Blow Molding IML, which involves forming hollow products; and Thermoforming IML, used for shaping thin plastic sheets. These processes enhance product aesthetics and durability.</p></p>
