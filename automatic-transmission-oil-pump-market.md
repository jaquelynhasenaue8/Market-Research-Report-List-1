<p><ul><li>Internal Gear Oil Pump</li><li>Rotary Oil Pump</li><li>Blade Oil Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic transmission oil pump market comprises three main types: internal gear oil pumps, rotary oil pumps, and blade oil pumps. Internal gear oil pumps utilize meshing gears to create fluid flow, providing high efficiency and reliability. Rotary oil pumps operate by rotating mechanisms to move fluid, offering compact designs suitable for various applications. Blade oil pumps use blades to draw and push oil, ensuring smooth operation and adaptability. Each type serves specific needs in automotive systems, contributing to efficient transmission performance.</p></p>
