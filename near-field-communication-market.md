<p><ul><li>Card Emulation</li><li>Reader Emulation</li><li>Peer-to-peer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Near Field Communication (NFC) market types include Card Emulation, Reader Emulation, and Peer-to-Peer. In Card Emulation, mobile devices act like contactless cards, enabling payments and access control. Reader Emulation allows devices to function as NFC readers, facilitating transactions or data exchange with NFC tags. Peer-to-Peer enables two NFC-enabled devices to exchange data directly, such as sharing files or connecting to services. Each type enhances user interaction and convenience in various applications, from payments to information sharing.</p></p>
