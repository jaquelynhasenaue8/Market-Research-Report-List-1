<p><p>The Consumer MEMS Inertial Sensors market encompasses three primary types: accelerometers, gyroscopes, and magnetometers. Accelerometers measure linear acceleration, enabling motion detection and orientation in devices like smartphones and fitness trackers. Gyroscopes assess angular velocity, facilitating stabilization and navigation, crucial for applications in drones and gaming controllers. Magnetometers gauge magnetic fields, often used for compass functionalities in mobile devices. Together, these sensors enhance user experiences in consumer electronics, enabling advanced features in navigation, gaming, and fitness tracking.</p></p>
