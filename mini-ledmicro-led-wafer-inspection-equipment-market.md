<p><p>The Mini LED/Micro LED wafer inspection equipment market includes two primary types: non-contact and contact inspection methods. Non-contact type equipment employs techniques such as optical or laser scanning to evaluate wafers without physical interaction, ensuring minimal risk of damage. In contrast, contact type equipment involves direct physical contact with the wafer surface, enabling detailed analysis of defects and surface characteristics. Both methods are crucial for ensuring quality and performance in the production of advanced LED technologies.</p></p>
